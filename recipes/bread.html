Big Dog Bread Recipe

Description

This will make 1 delicious loaf of bread

Ingredients

  • 450g of bread flour
  • 50g of all purpose flour
  • 5g of instant yeast
  • 12g of salt
  • 100ml of water

Steps

  1. Mix all of the ingrediants in a large bowl
  2. Allow the dough to rest for 2 hours
  3. Preheat oven to 500 degrees fahrenheit
  4. Place dough in dutch oven and place it in the oven for 40 minutes
  5. Remove dutch oven from the oven and take the bread out
  6. Allow the bread to cool to reoom temperture before eating
